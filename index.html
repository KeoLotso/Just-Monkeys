Code of Conduct

Welcome to the Just Monkeys community! To ensure a fun and respectful experience for everyone, we ask all players to follow these guidelines:

  • 1. Respect others and avoid offensive behavior.
  • 2. Do not exploit any bugs or glitches to gain an unfair advantage.
  • 3. Maintain a positive and welcoming community environment.
  • 4. No cheating, hacking, or using third-party tools to alter gameplay.
  • 5. not engage in any form of harassment, including bullying, spamming, or hate speech.
  • 6. Be mindful of other players' experiences and avoid disruptive actions.
  • 7. Do not impersonate other players or falsely represent your affiliation with the game or its developers.
  • 8. Keep all content shared in-game appropriate for all audiences.
  • 9. Follow all relevant local laws and regulations regarding online behavior.
  • 10. Report any violations of this Code of Conduct to the moderators or support team.

Failure to follow these guidelines may result in penalties, including temporary or permanent bans from the game. Let's work together to create a fun and respectful gaming environment for everyone!
